# 🤝 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Weak Signal Finder is a small, open project built to help people spot emerging themes in the news before they become mainstream. Whatever brings you here, curiosity about NLP, a specific monitoring use case, or just poking around the code, everyone should be able to participate without fear of harassment, discrimination, or bad-faith behavior.

We pledge to make participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## ✅ Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps this project thrive:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences, especially around contested topics that come up naturally in a media-monitoring tool.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback on issues and pull requests.
- Focusing on what's best for the project and its users, not just for yourself.
- Showing empathy toward other contributors, remember that text lacks tone, assume good faith.
- Being patient with newcomers, everyone was new to this codebase once.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private.
- Trolling, deliberately inflammatory or off-topic comments, or sustained disruption of discussions.
- Publishing others' private information (email, physical address, etc.) without explicit permission.
- Sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

---

## 🌍 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and commit messages, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces (e.g., posting from an official project account, or acting as an appointed representative at an event).

---

## 🛠️ Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by emailing:

**📧 littleviewer@proton.me**

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and treated with as much confidentiality as the situation reasonably allows. When reporting, please include:

- What happened and where (link to the issue, PR, or comment if applicable).
- Who was involved.
- Any additional context that would help evaluate the situation.

### Possible outcomes

| Response | When it applies |
|---|---|
| **Private warning** | First-time, lower-severity violations. A clear explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. |
| **Temporary interaction ban** | Repeated violations after a warning, or a single more serious incident. |
| **Permanent ban** | Sustained or severe violations, including harassment, threats, or repeated bad faith after prior warnings. |

Maintainers are also held to this Code of Conduct and are expected to lead by example. Anyone can report a maintainer's conduct using the same contact above.
